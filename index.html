本隐私政策说明"早睡"应用（以下简称"本应用"）如何收集、使用和分享你的信息。

1. 我们收集的信息

  • 账号信息：当你使用 Apple 或 Google 登录时，我们会收到来自相应平台的电子邮件地址和基本资料（如显示名称）。
  • 睡眠数据：本应用记录你手动输入的入睡打卡时间、睡眠目标，以及经你明确授权后从 Apple 健康读取的睡眠数据。
  • 设备交互信号：本应用会在你的目标入睡时间附近检测应用是否仍在被使用，以判断你是否仍未入睡并发送提醒。该数据仅在设备本地处理，不会上传到服务器。

2. 信息用途

  • 提供核心功能：睡眠提醒、每日打卡、连续天数统计、睡眠数据回顾。
  • 支持好友督促功能：你的入睡状态（是否按时入睡）可被你主动添加的好友查看。
  • 维护账号并跨设备同步个人资料。

3. 信息分享

  • 好友：你的入睡状态（按时/偏晚）对你主动添加的好友可见。详细睡眠记录和统计数据仅你本人可见。
  • 服务提供商：我们使用 Supabase 安全存储账号和社交数据，Supabase 作为数据处理方代表我们运营。
  • 我们不会将你的个人信息出售给第三方，也不会与广告商共享。

4. Apple 健康

如果你授予权限，本应用将从 Apple 健康读取睡眠数据以自动记录入睡时间。该数据仅用于提供应用功能，不会与第三方共享。

5. 数据保留与删除

你可以随时在应用内删除账号（设置 → 删除账号）。账号删除后，存储在我们服务器上的账号、资料及社交数据将被永久清除。存储在设备本地的睡眠记录不受影响。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，我们不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

7. 安全

我们采用行业标准加密和 Supabase 提供的安全基础设施保护你的信息。

8. 政策变更

我们可能不定期更新本隐私政策，重大变更将通过更新生效日期的方式通知你。

Privacy Policy

App Name: 早睡 (Sleep Early)
Effective Date: April 10, 2026
Developer: Irene Zhang
Contact: irene.studio@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy describes how 早睡 ("the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your email address and basic profile information (such as display name) from those providers.
  • Sleep data: The App records sleep check-in times and sleep goals that you manually enter, or that are read from Apple Health with your explicit permission.
  • Device interaction signals: The App detects whether it is actively being used near your target bedtime to determine if you are still awake, in order to send timely reminders. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core App features: sleep reminders, daily check-ins, streak tracking, and sleep statistics.
  • To enable the friend accountability feature: your sleep status (whether you slept on time) may be shared with friends you have explicitly added within the App.
  • To maintain your account and sync your profile across devices.

3. Information Sharing

  • Friends: Your sleep status (on time / late) is visible to friends you have explicitly added. Detailed sleep records and statistics are only visible to you.
  • Service providers: We use Supabase to store account and social data securely. Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties or share it with advertisers.

4. Apple Health

If you grant permission, the App reads sleep data from Apple Health to automatically record your sleep onset time. This data is used solely to provide the App's features and is not shared with third parties.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the App (Settings → Delete Account). Upon deletion, your account, profile, and social data stored on our servers will be permanently removed. Local sleep records stored on your device will not be affected.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information using industry-standard encryption and secure infrastructure provided by Supabase.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date above.
